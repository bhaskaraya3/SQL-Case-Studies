## Key Business Insights

• A small number of products and categories contribute disproportionately
  to total revenue, indicating strong portfolio concentration.

• Certain ship modes deliver faster but do not always generate higher revenue,
  highlighting an opportunity to optimize logistics cost vs value.

• Consumer and Corporate segments show higher average order value,
  making them priority targets for revenue growth.

• Regional analysis reveals uneven market penetration,
  suggesting expansion opportunities in underperforming states.

• Customer revenue concentration indicates dependency risk
  on a limited number of high-value customers.
